Mission

TechBioSA bridges the gap between traditional life sciences and cutting-edge technology — building the community that will accelerate Saudi Arabia's biotech future.

Vision

To position the Kingdom at the forefront of the global TechBio revolution, connecting scientists, engineers, and innovators aligned with Vision 2030.

What is TechBio?

TechBio applies AI, machine learning, and automation to biological systems — fundamentally transforming how we discover and develop drugs and therapies.

iv.

Why Saudi Arabia?

With strong investment in healthcare infrastructure and a growing scientific talent pool, KSA is uniquely positioned to leapfrog into the next era of biotech.
