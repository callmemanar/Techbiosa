What is TechBio?

TechBio applies AI, machine learning, and automation to biological systems — fundamentally transforming how we discover and develop drugs and therapies. It sits at the intersection of computer science and life science, building tools that compress decades of research into years.

Why Saudi Arabia?

With bold investment in healthcare infrastructure, a young scientific talent pool, and the ambition of Vision 2030, KSA is uniquely positioned to leapfrog into the next era of biotech — not follow, but lead.
